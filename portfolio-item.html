A short subtitle

I believe that giving back to the society that raised you is the best gift you could give - which is why I enjoy volunteering - be this online or in person.

Through taking part in a weekly mentoring scheme named Zero Gravity - I am able to provide application and educational support to young people from under-represented backgrounds to secure a place at a top university of their choice. Through working with my mentee on their personal statement, mock exams, additional interviews and entrance exams - I help to build upon not only their abilities, but their confidence.
