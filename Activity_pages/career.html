The Great British Career Search

Networking, experience and opportunity

For me, it’s important that I set myself up with the best possible chance to work in a job that is meaningful, impactful and that I enjoy! In order to achieve this, I am constantly searching for opportunities to gain insights and experience within the software development workplace to both prepare me for the sector and so that I am able to provide the best possible service when I graduate and begin my full-time job.

To do this, I enjoy making the most out of LinkedIn, BrightNetwork, GradCracker and other platforms to attend seminars, career fairs, apply for internships and insight weeks – all of which teach me skills I can implement in my course and in the future. However, I believe the best part of this is making new connections with people, finding out about their lives and watching this grow into friendship.
