Fitness and music

For me, it is essential to allow for downtime and engage in non-educational activities that I equally love – a big one for me is strength training. Through fitness and strength training in particular, I am able to focus on and work towards non-academic goals while making the best use of additional (nervous) energy! Physical activities such as this improve both my physical and mental strength – but most importantly make me happy.

On the opposite end, I love to relax by listening to and playing the ukulele – since I received mine as a gift from a friend, I have not put it down! Since the instrument is relatively easy to pick up, I am able to learn new chords, strumming patterns and songs with ease (although I can’t say the same about the singing!).
